Privacy Policy

At TruBoard Technologies, we prioritize the trust you place in us. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our platform and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email, phone, and company details.
  • Usage information: activity, features accessed, and interaction data.
  • Device information: browser, OS, and basic technical identifiers.
  • Transaction information: purchase and billing-related records.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve services.
  • To process transactions and send confirmations.
  • To share product updates, support notices, and security alerts.
  • To respond to user requests and provide customer support.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only with consent, for service delivery, or when legally required.

4. Data Security

  • Encrypted transport for sensitive workflows.
  • Strict access controls and role-based permissions.
  • Operational and contractual confidentiality safeguards.

5. Cookie Policy

Cookies and similar technologies are used for authentication, session continuity, performance, and user experience improvement.

6. Acceptable Use

Users must not misuse platform access, attempt unauthorized access, or transmit malicious content through TruBoard systems.
